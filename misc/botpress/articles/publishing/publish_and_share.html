Publishing and sharing a bot

Publishing

Once you are satisfied with your bot, you can publish it by clicking the Publish button on the top right of the studio page.

Botpress will take between 10 seconds and 3 minutes to gather your bot's data, connect with the Webchat framework, and publish your bot.

Sharing

Once your bot is published, you will be provided with a shareable link that lets you access the bot from its own unique site.

You can find this link at any time by clicking the Share button at the top right of the studio page. The Share button also gives you the option to customize your bots appearance.

Troubleshooting

Why did my bot fail to publish?

If you can't publish your bot, there may be an internet connection issue or an internal server error. Try these steps to resolve it:

  1. Refresh the page and retry
  2. Check your internet connection and retry
  3. Ensure Botpress servers are online
  4. Look at the error messages for guidance

Why doesn't the shared link work?

If you are having trouble accessing the shareable webchat, it could be an issue with the link or the publishing process. Try these steps:

  1. Try waiting and refreshing the page you accessed via the link
  2. Try generating a new link and using it
  3. Try republishing your bot and resharing a link. It may take a minute to be accessible by the shared link, even after clicking publish. Then test the bot's website

Why is my bot not responding?

If the webchat bot isn't responding to your input, it could be an error with the publishing process, the shared link, or your price plan.

  1. Check if you have exceeded your price plan limit. If you have, consider upgrading to a higher price limit
  2. Try waiting and refreshing the page
  3. Try republishing the bot
  4. Try using a new shareable link

Why is my bot exhibiting unexpected behaviour?

Unexpected behaviour is often the result of an error in the workflow configuration. To solve this, you can:

  1. Check your bot's workflows to ensure they are programmed correctly. Follow the Workflows and Cards guides for more assistance

Further issues

If you are still experiencing issues, talk with our automated troubleshooting representative via the chat icon in the bottom right corner, or contact Botpress' team through their Discord.
