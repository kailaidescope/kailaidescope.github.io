What is Botpress?

Botpress is an open-source platform designed to create, manage, and extend chatbots. It provides a comprehensive set of tools and features to help developers build conversational interfaces for various applications.

Signing up for Botpress

Welcome to Botpress! To get started, visit our registration page.

Fill in your details, including your name, email, and password. Make sure to choose a strong password to keep your account secure.

After submitting the form, check your email for a confirmation link. Click the link to verify your email address and complete the sign-up process. You're now ready to use Botpress!

Registering with Google

To sign up for Botpress using your Google account, visit our registration page and select the "Sign up with Google" option. Follow the prompts to log in with your Google credentials and authorize Botpress to access your account.
