Creating a bot

Instructions

When logged into the dashboard, go to the home screen by clicking the Home button in the top left corner. Once on the home screen, click +Create Bot in the center pane.

A loading bar will appear as the bot is initialized.

Once the bot is initialized, you will be prompted with a Chatbot created pop-up, allowing you to access the bot through the studio.

Troubleshooting

Where can I see the "+Create Bot" button?

Navigate to the Home menu, using the top left sidebar, within the dashboard. The +Create Bot button will be visible in the center pane.

Why can't I create a new bot?

You may have reached your bot limit for your Botpress account.

Basic accounts are limited to 1 bot, which can be increased with an upgraded pricing plan.

Further issues

If you are still experiencing issues, talk with our automated troubleshooting representative via the chat icon in the bottom right corner, or contact Botpress' team through their Discord.
