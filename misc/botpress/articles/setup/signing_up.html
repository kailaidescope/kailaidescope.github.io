Signing up for Botpress

Instructions

To sign up for an account, go to Botpress' Get Started page.

After following the on-screen prompts to create your account, you will receive a verification email. If the email doesn't appear in your inbox, make sure to check your spam folder.

Click the link in the verification email to complete your account setup. Now you're ready to build bots.

Troubleshooting

Why am I getting a duplicate account error?

This error means you already signed up for a Botpress account with a different provider (e.g. you tried signing up with your email but you already signed up through Google).

To resolve this, find the existing provider by searching your emails, or by trying to log in with different providers (e.g. try Google if you can't sign in with Facebook).

When searching your emails, look for messages from noreply@botpress.com and noreply@botpress.cloud.

Why does it say my email is unverified?

Botpress sends a verification email upon account registration. If your email is unverified, you can verify your email by checking your inbox.

You should look for an email from noreply@botpress.cloud with the headline "Verify your Botpress Cloud account". Then, click the link in the email body to complete the verification process.

Further issues

If you are still experiencing issues, talk with our automated troubleshooting representative via the chat icon in the bottom right corner, or contact Botpress' team through their Discord.
