Accessing the bot studio

Note: You can only have one studio session open per Workspace

Instructions

If you have created a bot and want to access its editor studio, first follow the steps to access the dashboard.

Then, find the bot's name on the home screen of the dashboard, hover over it, and click Edit in Studio.

You will be redirected to the studio, where you can modify, test, and publish your bot.

The studio takes around 10 seconds to load, as it collects all your bot's data.

Once you are in the studio, you can use it to customize the behaviour of the bot using the emulator, your workflows, and your knowledge bases.

Bot Setup

If your bot has not been set up yet, you will be prompted to choose a template for it and go through setup steps.

If you need help setting up the bot but have left the setup screen, click the Assistant button at the top center of the screen to get help.

Troubleshooting

Where can I find my bot?

Make sure you are in the home menu, which can be accessed through the Home button on the left sidebar.

If the bot is not there, try looking in other workspaces, which you can access by clicking the top left corner of the dashboard.

If you still can't find the bot, check the activity logs on the right sidebar to see if it was deleted.

Why can't I open the studio?

You may have another studio session open in the same Workspace. Since only one studio session is allowed per workspace, check if you have another session open, and close it.

A studio session is another way to say an open window on the studio page.

If you don't have another studio session open in the same Workspace, try the following steps:

  1. Close the studio, refresh your dashboard page, and reopen the studio
  2. Restart your browser and open the studio again
  3. Try making a new Workspace (in the top left of the dashboard), and making a new bot. If you can open this bot's studio, try waiting for 1 minute and re-opening the original bot's studio

Further issues

If you are still experiencing issues, talk with our automated troubleshooting representative via the chat icon in the bottom right corner, or contact Botpress' team through their Discord.
