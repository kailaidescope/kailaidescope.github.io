Accessing the dashboard

Instructions

First, login using the account you created. You will be redirected to the dashboard when you are logged in.

It is worth noting some key elements of the dashboard's user interface. The key elements are listed by location:

  • Top-left: Account information and current workspace
  • Left: Bot-specific information
  • Center: Overview of bots in this workspace
  • Right: Overview of usage and recent activity

Note: Bots exist in only one of your workspaces. To change workspaces, click the top left corner of the dashboard. You can make a new workspace through the same method.

Troubleshooting

Why can't I log in?

To fix login problems follow these steps:

  1. Ensure you are logging in through the right account provider (e.g. email, Facebook, Google). To find your provider, check your email's inbox for an account verification email and look at the details.
  2. Ensure you are using the right password. If you forgot your password, try resetting it.

Why am I not redirected to the dashboard upon login?

If you are logged in already, use this link to access the dashboard.

If the link redirects you to the login page, try logging in again, then click the dashboard link again.

Further issues

If you are still experiencing issues, talk with our automated troubleshooting representative via the chat icon in the bottom right corner, or contact Botpress' team through their Discord.
